Spotify Playlist Generator

Discover new music from your followed artists and their related artists. Generate playlists with the latest releases from the past week!

How it works:
Scans your followed artists on Spotify
Explores "Fans also like" related artists
Finds releases from the past 7 days
Creates a personalized playlist for you
Connect with Spotify
Your data is secure and we only access what's needed to create your playlist
Setup Instructions
Before you start:
  1. Make sure you have a Spotify account
  2. Follow some artists you like on Spotify
  3. The app will need permission to create playlists
What you'll get:
  • A private playlist with recent discoveries
  • Songs from the last 7 days
  • Music from artists similar to your taste
  • Automatic playlist naming with date
